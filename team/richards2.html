Jenner Richards - Ph.D, Chief Technology Officer

Jenner Richards is a Mechanical Engineer with aerospace experience in both manned and unmanned aircraft design and testing. Previous experience has ranged from the design of an aircraft fuel system for a deHavilland Beaver turboprop conversion, to FE analysis of aircraft structures in support of Transport Canada certification. Technical Lead in the US Air Force/Virginia Tech Sensorcraft project.

Chief Information Officer

Shohreh Hadian, MSc, is a Faculty in Computer Science, Camosun College, BC, Canada.  She holds a BSc (Honours) in Information System from City University, Washington, USA and MSc in Computer Science from University of Victoria, BC. Shohreh brings 30 years of experience in systems design and management, software development, database design, and teaching to the company. Since 2004, Shohreh has been a member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers; and a member of the Association for Computing Machinery

Shohre is responsible for Information technology management and computer system that support enterprise goal. Setting objective and strategies for the enterprise. selecting and implementing suitable technology to streamline all internal operations and help optimize their strategic benefits Design and customize technological systems and platforms to improve customer experience.

Quaternion TelemViewer® Software

Quaternion TelemViewer Software provides real time visualization of aircraft states and telemetry to augment existing commercial Ground Control Software. TelemView interfaces with the GCS data stream and can output the aircrafts position and attitude to interface or to your choice of commercial and open source Flight Simulators (Xplane*, and Flight Gear*). Some features include:

  • Interfaces available for Picollo and Micropilot GCS (contact us for custom solution)
  • Choice of live telemetry mode or replay logged flight data
  • Aircraft states can be visualized in custom interface, X-plane or Flight Gear
  • Telemetry viewing with aircraft gauges and text. Some examples Aircraft altitude above launch, GPS/Barometric Altitude •Artificial horizon, climb rate, bearing gauges •Aircraft health information such as battery voltages and control state.

Co-Operative Education
Internship Program

Quaternion Aerospace cooperative education and internship program attempts to strengthen the link between the academic community and the professional engineering and business world through practical, hands-on experience. Our internship program provides a focused program for the growth and development of students in an engineering business environment. This program represents one of the many ways Quaternion Aerospace encourages the continual education of students and engineers at all levels. With this program, Quaternion Aerospace ultimately seeks to contribute positively to the community as well as to the development of future scientists and engineers.

QT2 Platform

Specifications at a Glace

Wing Span 3.2m (126inches)

Wing Area 1.2m2 (1860 inches2)

Payload Capacity 5.5kg (12.1 lb)

Payload Dimensions 52cm (L) x 16 cm (H) x 16cm (W) or 20.5”(L)x6.3”(H)x6.3”(W)

Weight (Dry) 5.8kg (12.8 lb)

Features

Pusher configuration ideal for forward looking camera payloads

Longer endurance platform than QT1 with up to 11hr endurance

Full composite construction with stable high-wing design, available as electric and 4-stroke gas propulsion

Large volume, easily accessible payload bay with internal payload mounting rails and optional isolated mount for forward facing camera payloads

Available as airframe only or as deployable UAS

Description

The QT2 platform is designed as a remote sensing platform with extended range and endurance. The pusher configuration allows for wide variety of forward facing camera/sensor options. The platform offers a reliable, cost effective solution for applications including UAV development, surveillance and remote sensing.

The QT2 is available in three configurations
Option 1: Airflrame only

The QT2 is available as an airframe only and is ready for avionics, engine and servo integration.

Option 2: Airframe with engine, servos and fuel tanks integrated

This option is almost ready to fly with 4-stroke engine, servos and fuel tank pre-installed.

Option 3: Flight ready UAS

This option is flight ready in under an hour with the integrated 4-stroke engine, fuel tanks and servos and the choice of the Quaternion’s ezAP® or ezAP-MARINE® Autopilots. The package includes the custom Ground Control Software (Laptop not included) and each system has been test flown and the autopilot tuned and tested for typical usage scenarios. (Other COTS autopilots available upon request)
